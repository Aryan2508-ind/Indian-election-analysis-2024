Key Analysis Findings
BJP Seat Drop
BJP won 280 seats — securing a strong majority above the 272 mark. Identified via groupby + value_counts() on cleaned party column after alias normalization.
INC Recovery
INC nearly doubled its tally to 103 seats from 52 in 2019. Detected via merge with 2019 baseline CSV and swing_vs_2019 computed feature column.
UP Swing
Uttar Pradesh saw the largest swing — SP+INC won 41 of 80 seats, up from 6 in 2019. Computed via state-wise groupby aggregation and Pearson r = 0.71 vs 2022 assembly results.
High Turnout States
Tripura (84.8%), Lakshadweep (84.2%) and West Bengal (80.3%) led turnout. Binned via pd.cut() into Low / Medium / High categories post null-imputation.
